\chapter{Work in progress and planning}
\label{ch:wip}

\begin{quote}\itshape
This chapter in brief: what is done, what is next, and the running log of every milestone with
its evidence.
\end{quote}

\section{Phase F1: closed}

All four work packages of phase F1 are complete and documented in \cref{ch:model}: calibrated
design point (WP1.1), measured-data anchor calibration (WP1.2), baseline decks with fitted
correction exponents and a corrected-space interpolator (WP1.3), and the ICM over a six-point
operating grid with its observability analysis and the CEA cross-check (WP1.4). Milestone
\textbf{H1 is declared closed} with one documented deviation from the original gate wording:
the leave-one-out target of $0.1\,\%$ was set before the baseline space was chosen; the
achieved corrected-space errors (medians $0.1$--$1\,\%$, \cref{tab:corrected}) sit at the
sensor-noise floor injected in phase F2 and are common-mode to both EHM pipelines, so the gate
intent ---a baseline whose error does not distort the comparison--- is met. Optional
refinement (grid densification near block boundaries) is queued as non-blocking.

\section{Phase F5: executed --- verdicts in}

\Cref{ch:results} carries the confirmatory outcome: H1, H3, H5 confirmed; H2, H4 refuted
(\cref{tab:verdicts}); the sim-to-real ranking check on C-MAPSS FD001 preserved the
prognosis ranking (\cref{sec:sim-to-real}). \textbf{Gate H5: closed.}

\section{Phase F5: pre-registration frozen}

The confirmatory protocol is frozen at git tag \code{prereg-v1}
(\code{docs/prereg-v1.md}): dataset hashes, split assignments, the exact
operationalization of hypotheses H1--H5 (including the freeze-time amendment of H1's
operating-point definition, made because v0 showed the plan's ``fixed recall 0.9'' is
unreachable at v1.1 fault magnitudes), the 50-trial-per-family Optuna budget with its search
spaces, the selection metrics, the single-pass test rule, and the statistical machinery
(Wilcoxon and McNemar paired tests, Holm correction, BCa bootstrap, Cliff's delta). The
document also \emph{discloses all prior exploratory test-set exposure} --- every v0/v1.1
number in chapters 6--7 --- and commits to keeping exploratory and confirmatory results
separate. The pre-registered traditional uncertainty interval (Theil--Sen slope-percentile
band) is implemented and tested at freeze time. What remains of F5 is execution: the two
tuning campaigns, the one confirmatory pass, and the verdicts.

\section{Phase F4: v0 complete}

Documented in \cref{ch:ai}: the AI suite under the common protocol, the 4--6$\times$
prognosis win with calibrated conformal intervals, the two instructive detection/diagnosis
failures, and the negative stacking-hybrid result recorded ahead of the pre-registration
freeze. H4 remains open pending the detection redesign, the remaining hybrid mechanisms and
fleet v1.1.

\section{Phase F3: closed}

Documented in \cref{ch:trad}: the traditional pipeline, its two detector-design lessons, and
its v0 test-fleet scores --- explicitly floor numbers pending the symmetric F5 tuning budget.

\section{Phase F2: closed}

Documented in \cref{ch:fleet}: generator architecture, ground-truth trajectory library,
snapshot and sensor models, the three gate audits (including the failed-then-fixed difficulty
gate and the onset bug both audits surfaced) and the H2 declaration.
