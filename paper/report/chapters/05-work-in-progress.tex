\section{Closing phase F1}

Two work packages remain to close milestone H1:

\begin{description}
  \item[WP1.3 --- Baseline decks.] Sweep the model over the operating envelope (altitude
    $\times$ Mach $\times$ thrust setting $\times$ $\Delta T_{\mathrm{ISA}}$ $\times$ bleed)
    and package a healthy-engine interpolator validated by leave-one-out (interpolation error
    $<0.1$\,\%). This interpolator is the baseline against which both EHM pipelines will
    compute measurement deviations.
  \item[WP1.4 --- Influence coefficient matrix.] Central finite differences ($\pm0.5$\,\%) on
    the ten health parameters over a sub-grid of operating conditions; an SVD analysis (rank,
    condition number, angles between fault signatures) that defines the ``confusable'' subset
    of hypothesis H2; automated physical sign checks (e.g.\
    $\Delta\eta_{\mathrm{HPT}}\downarrow \Rightarrow \mathrm{EGT}\uparrow, W_f\uparrow$); and
    the tabular-versus-CEA thermodynamics cross-check at the anchor points.
\end{description}
